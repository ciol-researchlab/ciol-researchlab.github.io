Multilingual Legal AI Assistant for Bangladesh

Our plan is to develop a human-advocate level Multilingual Legal AI Assistant, specialized in Bangladesh Law or Legal System.

What is the question we want to answer?

The main goal of our project is to make legal advice and assistance in Bangladesh more accessible for everyone. Right now, the legal system is slow, costly, and often difficult to navigate. Many people donâ€™t fully understand their rights or feel discouraged from seeking justice due to the systemâ€™s complexity or the expense of hiring a good lawyer. We want to develop an AI system that can help overcome these issues by providing legal support to both legal professionals and the public. We believe an AI legal assistant can make legal services faster, more affordable, and more reliable.

Why is this question important?

This is important because the current legal system in Bangladesh has several problems. Many people are unaware of their legal rights, and even when they do know their rights, they often feel hesitant to pursue justice due to high costs, fear of bias, or the difficulty of finding a good lawyer. An AI assistant could help solve these issues by making the legal process more straightforward and accessible to everyone, no matter their background or financial situation.
