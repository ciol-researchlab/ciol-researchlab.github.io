2022 CIOL is founded by MD Shafikul Islam and Azmine Toushik Wasi, to accelerate OR-AI combinational research at SUST-IPE, filling up the current gap of ML-foocused research in the department. Fueled by love for discovery and innovation, it's a student-centric research lab dedicated to training and empowering young researchers in OR, CO, and ML for interesting and impactful projects benefiting different industries, broadly humanity.
